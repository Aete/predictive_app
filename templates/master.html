Work Zone Collision Probability Prediction

This is a k-means clustering based predictive application to calculate the collision probability of a crash within 900 feet of a roadway construction work zone. For more information visit this website.

How to use?

  • 1. Click a segment corresponding the planned work location
  • 2. Choose a create and close time of planned work
  • 3. Click 'Submit'
  • 4. Click the polygons on the map

Note

  • Total work duration must be less than 24 hours
  • Planned work must be on a 'Street', 'Highway', 'Bridge', 'Tunnel', or 'Ramp'
